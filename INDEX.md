## 📧 EMAIL SYSTEM OVERVIEW

When someone uses your contact form:

```
1. Client fills form (name, email, subject, message)
2. JavaScript sends to EmailJS servers
3. EmailJS authenticates with your credentials
4. EmailJS formats email using template
5. Email sent to your inbox
6. You get notification email
7. You click Reply
8. Client receives your response
```

**Result:** Direct client communication, no database needed!
